Of Life and Humans

Each one of us is bound by life. And life is bound by time. Time is limited and therefore what you do with time is important.
Everyday, you come across something new or someone new. You learn from each of these interactions. These interactions may be time bound but the learnings extend beyond time. These interactions build bonds and relationships. Some of these bonds are time bound while some aren’t. All of these interactions have you in the centre.
What if you, being time bound, cease to exist? What happens to the bonds? What happens to the learnings?

About Me

Life is not linear. I learnt this through my experience in life. I graduated in Chemistry but worked in a bank. Many years later, I opted for voluntary retirement. Post this began my engagement with the world of insurance.
I started out as an agent for life insurance ten years ago. Currently, I am qualified as an agent for insurance as well as for mutual funds.
This journey has been one with ups and downs. Each day is teaches me a new lesson. I have learnt to be patient and accept everything that comes my way without complaining. My desire to reach a newer height has been growing as each day passes by. This has been possible because I of the support I have gained from people and the insurance cover I have.
Hence, I would like whoever is reading this to join me in this journey of creating a society where each one grows by lending a hand of support to those close to them. Let us create a network of support. Let us insure ourselves.
