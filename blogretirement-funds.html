Are retirement funds
the only resource you are relying on?

You may be retiring soon or in a few years. Are you planning to live the rest of your life on the funds you receive at the retirement?
A decade ago that might have been sufficient, but not today.
Here’s why you should not depend on your retirement funds alone.
  1. Longevity of life is longer: At the time of retirement, you may expect to live for another 20 years. What if you live beyond 80? This means that you will outlive the funds available. What will you do once you have exhausted your existing corpus?
  2. Healthcare costs: Health-related issues are much higher post retirement due to increasing age. You may be leading a healthy life currently, but considering human body which can be likened to a machine, the process of deterioration begins as age progresses. And therefore, healthcare costs become a routine. The cost of healthcare services is rising with each passing day. This means that relying on your retirement corpus alone would not cover the costs.
  3. Unexpected event: This may be in terms of medical emergencies or any other unexpected event like financing a close one’s education. Early withdrawal from retirement funds may leave less for later.
  4. Tax changes and inflation: Many choose annuity to utilise retirement funds. However, few years the annuity rates will be low to combat the higher rates due to inflation. Moreover, unexpected tax changes are possible. Therefore what you decide today may not hold good enough for tomorrow.

These are some of the issues that you face when you rely completely on your retirement funds. So what can you do to evade such a future?
Gain knowledge and plan your future wisely.
